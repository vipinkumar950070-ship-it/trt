Personal Data Protection Policy

Version: August 2024

This personal data protection policy aims to inform you of the terms of processing of your personal data within the framework of the Primestreams service.

Who is responsible for processing your personal data?

The controller of your personal data within the framework of the Service is Alchimie, SA with a board of directors and capital of €4,494,822 registered with the RCS under number 420 919 904, whose head office is located at 33 rue La Fayette 75009 Paris.

What personal data is processed?

Personal data we collect when you subscribe to a Primestreams subscription

  • Identification data: email address, password, technical customer identifier and possibly your Gmail or Facebook Token ID emails if you choose to connect through this means
  • Financial data when subscribing to a paid subscription: history (transaction date, amount paid, currency, payment method), then depending on your payment method: Bank card information, Paypal account information, information related to “direct carrier billing” (direct billing by the mobile operator) such as MSISDN

Personal data we collect when you use Primestreams

  • Usage data / Technical data: connection log on our site/applications, site/applications navigation data, audiovisual content consumption data, country of request for access to the service, IP address

Personal data we collect when you contact our customer service

  • Details of your request or complaint (including date, time, subject and content of your request), content of your exchanges with Customer Service, any other data considered necessary to process your request or complaint

For what purposes and on what legal bases?

Purposes

  • Create and manage your subscription: access to the Primestreams service, billing and collection of payments, management of options linked to your subscription, processing of your requests to customer service;
  • Send you communications relating to our service and our offers;
  • Improve the quality of our service: improving features, performance of the service, measuring and analyzing consumption and use of content included in Primestreams, communicating information about the service and its features, as well as our promotional offers;
  • Fulfilling our legal obligations.

Legal Bases

  • Execution of your subscription contract
  • Our legitimate interest in promoting our business and services, regarding communications relating to products and offers similar to those to which you have access as part of the subscription you have taken out. In other cases, the legal basis will be your prior consent.
  • Our legitimate interest in measuring and improving services and products to ensure the sustainability of our activities.
  • The execution of a legal obligation.

For what durations?

Your personal data is strictly confidential and kept for a period that varies depending on the purposes set out in the previous article, in accordance with the applicable regulations. After this period, your data will be deleted or anonymized.

Where is your personal data processed and to whom is it transmitted?

All of Alchimie's servers hosting your data are located in the European Union; the latter does not transfer any of your data outside this zone.

Furthermore, Alchimie may use subcontractors as part of the Primestreams service, and your data may also be transmitted to service providers responsible for:

  • The capture and encoding of linear services
  • Billing and payment of your subscription
  • Customer support management
  • Sending newsletters and commercial proposals
  • Analysis of subscriber usage and marketing operations

How do we protect them?

Alchimie has implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against destruction, loss, alteration or unauthorized disclosure.

We implement all useful means to guarantee the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your data, we ensure to store data concerning you in information systems offering an appropriate level of security, access to which is protected, restricted and recorded.

What are your rights and how to exercise them?

In accordance with applicable regulations, you have the right to access, rectify and delete personal data concerning you.

You can request the portability of this data. You also have the right to object to the processing or to request its limitation. Finally, you have the right to define guidelines regarding the fate of your data after your death.

To exercise your rights, we invite you to contact us by email at the following address: rgpd@primestreams.pro
